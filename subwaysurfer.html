About Subway Surfer

Subway Surfer is an endless runner mobile game developed by Kiloo and SYBO Games. It was released in 2012 and has become one of the most popular mobile games worldwide.

In the game, players control a character who runs along subway tracks, jumping over obstacles, collecting coins, and avoiding trains.

Game Features

Endless Running

Run forever through dynamic subway environments.

Collect Coins

Gather coins to unlock new characters and upgrades.

Avoid Obstacles

Jump, slide, and surf to dodge trains and barriers.

Multiple Characters

Choose from a variety of characters with unique abilities.
